hi,i'm

A first-generation, low-income sophomore at Brown University studying Computer Engineering with a focus on robotics and software engineering with experience building applications with Java, Python, Reactjs, HTML, CSS3, and a few other frameworks.

I am an advocate for socially responsible computing. I believe that technology can build and shape our future. As software developers and engineers, we must not only write clean and efficient code, but responsible code. We must think before we create.

This website, which I designed and coded from scratch, contains information about me, my projects, my organizations, and contact information.

about me

I grew up in Lawrence, Massachussetts after my parents immigrated from Vietnam in 1993. My fascination with technology began early on when I questioned how televisions worked and further developed through various engineering, robotics, and computer science programs in highschool. Through these programs I've gained valuable insight into these fields. One such insight is the social affects of an age of rising technology usage. These social affects range from mental health issues to privacy rights. As such, I am working on building socially responsible softwares to further integrate technology into our day-to-day lives in a balanced manner.

I am most comfortable with Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and React.js. Using these tech stacks, I have built websites, complex puzzle algortihms for games such as PacMan and Tetris, and solved real world problems. My full list of tech stacks can be found on my LinkedIn.
